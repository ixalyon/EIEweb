Abstract
Thanks to the developments in the telecommunication infrastructure; internet and services based on it are widely used in today's world. As a result, network security has become a critical issue due to the different cyber threats introduced everyday.  Therefore firewalls are increasingly used to ensure the security of networks. The primary job of a firewall is to protect a network from security threats and to enforce security policies. There are two types of firewalls available in the market: Software Firewalls and Hardware Firewalls. This paper discusses the design and the development of a hardware firewall with integrated packet analyzer which is targeted for the domestic use.
